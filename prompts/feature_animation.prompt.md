# feature animation

implemement animation

## types of animation logic

animations are called when updating the map screen

move animation

- played when player, item or enemy moves
- unique animation for moving up, down left, right, diagonal left, diagonal right

attack animation

- played when player or enemy attacks
- unique animation for attacking up, down left, right, diagonal left, diagonal right

spell animation

- played when player or enemy casts a spell
- unique animation for casting up, down left, right, diagonal left, diagonal right

suplex animation

- played when player or enemy suplexes
- unique animation for casting up, down left, right, diagonal left, diagonal right

suplexed animation

- played when player or enemy suplexes
- unique animation for casting up, down left, right, diagonal left, diagonal right

defend animation

hurt animation

die animation
